# Northbridge RiskGuard — Outreach Pack

## Positioning

Northbridge RiskGuard is a lightweight risk, responsible gaming and compliance workflow console for iGaming operators.

The goal of outreach is **validation**, not hard selling.

Ask for feedback from people who understand risk/compliance/fraud/payments workflows.

---

## LinkedIn message — Head of Risk / Compliance

Hi {{first_name}},

I’m validating a lightweight B2B tool for iGaming risk and compliance teams — Northbridge RiskGuard.

The idea is to help teams move from player events to structured review: risk flags, review queue, responsible gaming decisions, safe intervention templates, notes, audit trail and exportable reports.

I’m not trying to sell anything at this stage — I’m looking for 15–20 minutes of expert feedback from people who understand iGaming risk/compliance workflows.

Would you be open to a short feedback call next week?
