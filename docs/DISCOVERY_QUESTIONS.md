# Northbridge RiskGuard — Discovery Questions

Use these questions for validation calls with iGaming risk, compliance, responsible gaming, fraud, payments and operations people.

## 1. Role and ownership

1. What is your role in the risk / compliance / fraud / payments workflow?
2. Who owns responsible gaming review in your organization?
3. Who owns fraud / bonus abuse review?
4. Who owns payment-risk review?
5. Are these workflows handled by one team or several teams?

## 2. Current workflow

1. How do you currently detect high-risk player behavior?
2. What systems do analysts use daily?
3. Is the review workflow centralized or spread across BI, spreadsheets, CRM, back office and tickets?
4. How are cases created?
5. How are notes and decisions recorded?
6. Is there an audit trail?
7. How painful is reporting today?

## 3. Risk signals

Which signals matter most?

- deposit spike;
- loss chasing;
- long sessions;
- late-night sessions;
- high deposit frequency;
- failed withdrawals;
- bonus abuse;
- multi-accounting;
- chargebacks;
- KYC mismatch;
- payment method changes;
- self-exclusion indicators;
- support messages;
- reversal / cancelled withdrawal patterns.

Ask:

1. Which signals are false-positive heavy?
2. Which signals are most urgent?
3. Which signals are required by policy or regulation?
4. Which signals are commercially important but not compliance-driven?

## 4. Data and integration

1. What data sources are available?
2. Is CSV upload enough for a pilot?
3. Would API integration be mandatory?
4. What event fields are usually available?
5. How often should risk scoring run?
6. Is near-real-time needed?
7. Are anonymized datasets possible for vendor validation?

## 5. Case management

1. How are review cases assigned?
2. What statuses do you use?
3. What decisions do analysts make?
4. Do analysts need templates?
5. Do managers approve interventions?
6. Do cases need escalation?
7. What must appear in audit trail?

## 6. Responsible gaming interventions

1. What player-facing messages are allowed?
2. Who approves message templates?
3. What interventions are common?
4. Are limits suggested manually or automatically?
5. When is cooling-off recommended?
6. What should never be said to a player?
7. How do you avoid retention-style messaging in RG cases?

## 7. Reports

1. What reports are needed weekly/monthly?
2. Who reads the reports?
3. Are reports internal, regulator-facing, or both?
4. What evidence must be included?
5. Is PDF required?
6. Is CSV export needed?
7. What KPIs matter?

## 8. Buying and pilot

1. Who would sponsor a tool like this?
2. Which budget would it come from?
3. What would make this worth a paid pilot?
4. What would block adoption?
5. Is this a nice-to-have or urgent pain?
6. What price range would be reasonable for a pilot?
7. What would be a strong success metric after 2–4 weeks?

## 9. Demo feedback

After showing the demo:

1. What feels realistic?
2. What feels wrong?
3. What is missing?
4. What is unnecessary?
5. Which screen is most valuable?
6. Which screen would your team ignore?
7. What would you change first?

## 10. Final validation question

If this existed with your data and your policy templates, would your team use it?

If yes — why?

If no — why not?
