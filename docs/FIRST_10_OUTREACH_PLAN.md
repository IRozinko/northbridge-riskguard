# Northbridge RiskGuard — First 10 Outreach Plan

This document defines the first 10 companies to test Northbridge RiskGuard positioning with.

The goal is not immediate sales. The goal is to get feedback calls with people who understand iGaming risk, responsible gaming, compliance, fraud, payments or platform operations.

## Role-specific outreach messages

### Message for Head of Risk / Risk Manager

Hi {{first_name}},

I’m validating a lightweight workflow tool for iGaming risk teams — Northbridge RiskGuard.

The MVP focuses on moving from raw player events to structured review: risk flags, review queue, case decisions, notes, audit trail, and exportable risk/compliance report.

I’m not trying to sell a finished product at this stage. I’m looking for 15–20 minutes of feedback from people who understand real risk operations in iGaming.

Would you be open to a short feedback call next week?

### Message for Compliance / Responsible Gaming

Hi {{first_name}},

I’m validating Northbridge RiskGuard — a lightweight risk and responsible gaming workflow console for iGaming operators.

The idea is to help teams structure RG/risk reviews: behavioral flags, player profile, case status, policy-safe intervention templates, notes, audit trail and exportable report.

This is not a gambling product and I’m not selling anything yet — I’m looking for expert feedback from compliance/RG people.

Would you be open to a short 15–20 minute feedback call?

### Message for Fraud / Payments

Hi {{first_name}},

I’m validating a lightweight risk workflow console for iGaming operators.

The MVP includes signals like failed withdrawals, high deposit frequency, bonus abuse patterns, risk scoring, manual review queue, decision history and exportable reports.

Since your background is close to fraud/payments/risk operations, I’d appreciate your feedback on whether this reflects real operational pain.

Would you be open to a short feedback call?

### Message for Product Manager / Platform Provider

Hi {{first_name}},

I’m validating a lightweight workflow layer for iGaming platforms/operators — Northbridge RiskGuard.

It sits between raw player events and manual risk/RG review: configurable rules, player profiles, review queue, case decisions, intervention templates, notes, audit trail and report export.

I’m trying to understand whether this should be an operator tool, platform add-on, or managed-service layer.

Would you be open to a short 15–20 minute feedback call?

## Success criteria for the first 30 contacts

- 30 connection/message attempts.
- 5–8 replies.
- 3–5 feedback calls.
- 1–2 strong signals that the problem is real.
- At least one intro to a better-fit person.

## Red flags during validation

- “We already have this fully covered internally.”
- “CSV import is useless; API is mandatory from day one.”
- “RG and fraud cannot live in the same workflow.”
- “Reports are not useful unless jurisdiction-specific.”
- “No one would pay unless it is deeply integrated into the platform.”

## Green flags during validation

- “We currently do this in spreadsheets / BI / back office manually.”
- “Audit trail is painful.”
- “RG templates and case history are useful.”
- “We need a lightweight tool before deeper integration.”
- “Can you support our own rules and thresholds?”
- “Could this work with anonymized data?”
- “I know someone who should see this.”
